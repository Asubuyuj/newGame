Basic Rules

  • Your hand is closer to 21 than the dealerâ€™s AND not over 21
  • You will play against the Dealer(CPU)
  • Each player will get 2 cards
  • Each player will then take turns to either 'Hit' or 'Stand'
  • Hit: Get another card
  • Stand: Keep current cards and end turn
  • Bust: Card Values are over 21
  • Push: You and Dealer tie with same value

Cards

  • Number Cards are face value
  • Face Cards (J,Q,K) = 10
  • Ace(A) can be either 1 or 11, which ever benefits your hand best
